About

I design thoughtful digital experiences with clarity, intention, and visual calm. My work focuses on UI/UX Design, Web Interfaces, Branding, and Front-End implementation—bringing ideas to life in a clean, structured, and visually engaging way

Hello there

Hi, I'm Diana W Mwangi - a calm-minded creative developer crafting serene digital journeys

I’m a UI/UX and Front-End Developer passionate about clean layouts, meaningful aesthetics, and user-centered functionality. I believe design should feel natural—quiet, intentional, and easy to understand.

My approach blends research, minimalistic design, and structured development to transform ideas into functional digital products. Whether it’s a website, interface, or brand identity, my goal is to create work that is purposeful and visually calm.
